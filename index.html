About EmotionSense

Unlocking Emotions: Where Technology meets Feelings

EmotionSense is an innovative project leveraging advanced facial recognition technology to detect and interpret human emotions accurately. By analyzing facial expressions in real-time, EmotionSense provides invaluable insights into the emotional states of individuals, revolutionizing various fields such as psychology, marketing, human-computer interaction, and beyond.
